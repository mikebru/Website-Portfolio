Simply Sand Play

Virtual Sand Tray Therapy | 2021-Present

About the Project

Simply Sand Play is a secure and effective telehealth tool that allows counselors to practice sandtray therapy with clients in a virtual environment. Built in response to COVID-19 and the need for digital tools that allow therapists to engage younger audiences in play therapy.

I developed all aspects of the application and maintain updates and bug fixes submitted from users. Released on multiple platforms (WebGL, iOS, Windows, OSX) to ensure accessibility for as many users as possible.
