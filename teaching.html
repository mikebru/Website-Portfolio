Teaching Portfolio

As an Instructional Assistant Professor at Texas A&M University's College of Performance, Visualization and Fine Art, I teach a variety of courses focused on interactive design, digital media, and creative technology. My teaching philosophy emphasizes hands-on learning, creative problem-solving, and the integration of technical skills with artistic expression.

VIST 110

Design Innovation

Introduction to fundamental design issues, processes, and theories relevant to design resolution and the creation of new ideas for innovative design products and services by keeping human needs at the center of the development process; Overview of design research, prototyping, evaluation, and communication techniques to generate valuable insights for design innovation.

Key Topics

Design Principles Human Centered Design Problem Solving Iteration Prototyping

VIST 210

Time and Interaction

Students will learn the language of time-based and interactive storytelling, with a focus on the skills needed to build a strong narrative experience and highly effective visuals for use in linear and non-linear motion graphics, and interactive projects. Students are expected to produce their own creative artifacts that grapple with the techniques and concepts related to time and interaction.

Key Topics

Principles of Animation Linear Narratives Interactive Narratives Unreal Engine

VIST 205, 305, 405

Interactive Vertical Studio

Allows students to explore experiential and interactive design through the creation of a semester long project of their own design. We will focus equally on the design process as well as producing and deploying our designs to be engaged with. Projects can take the form of art installations, exhibitions, themed attractions, interior design, event design, activations, etc. We will emphasize the pre-visualization of spaces and use 3-D technology to make design choices prior to deployment. In this studio students will focus their energy on creating a substantive project that combines visual thinking, design research, multimodality, interactivity, and storytelling.

Key Topics

Team Project Based Interactive Design Creative Technology Branding Experience Design

VIST 289

Special Topics in TouchDesigner

Introduction to the fundamentals and practices of real-time interactive multimedia production through the use of the industry leading software TouchDesigner. Creation of interactive graphics, procedural visuals, data-driven systems and control systems through the use of node based programming. Topics include projection mapping, using sensors, creating user interfaces, interoperability, optimization and rendering. The course will consist of individual assignments to learn fundamentals and techniques with a culminating project at the end of the course.

Key Topics

Emerging Technologies TouchDesigner Interactive Media Creative Coding
