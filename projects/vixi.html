Vixi Unreal

USA, 2023-Present

About the Project

Vixi Unreal revolutionizes fan engagement at live events by seamlessly blending social media with stunning in-stadium displays. This innovative platform integrates content from major social networks, allows fans to share selfies on the big screen, and offers powerful moderation tools for event organizers.

Featuring customizable 3D environments powered by Unreal Engine 5, Vixi Unreal transforms venues into interactive spaces. From sports arenas to concert halls, it creates unforgettable experiences that make fans an integral part of the event.
